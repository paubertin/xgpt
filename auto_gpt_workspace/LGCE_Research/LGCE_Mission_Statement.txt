LGCE's mission is to create customized solutions that adapt to the specific needs of businesses, rather than forcing businesses to adapt to pre-existing software. They have a team of multidisciplinary engineers with expertise in various areas who work on developing highly scalable and customizable web and mobile solutions for businesses of all sizes. Their focus is on research and innovation, particularly in the area of portability of heavy processing on mobile devices. They offer services such as consulting, software development, and proof of concept prototyping. They have worked with clients in various industries, including water management.
